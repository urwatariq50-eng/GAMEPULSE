GTA VI Gets Massive Gameplay Reveal

Rockstar officially unveils new gameplay, smarter AI, improved graphics and an expanded Vice City.

Article

Rockstar Games has revealed brand-new gameplay footage for Grand Theft Auto VI. The trailer showcases improved vehicle physics, smarter NPC behaviour, expanded city exploration and dynamic weather.

Players will explore the modern version of Vice City while experiencing one of the biggest open worlds Rockstar has ever created.

The game is expected to become one of the biggest launches in gaming history.
