GTA VI Vehicles: Cars, Boats and More

Vehicles have always been an important part of Grand Theft Auto, and GTA VI continues that tradition with an impressive selection shown in official trailers.

Sports Cars

The trailers feature modern sports cars inspired by real-world manufacturers, with detailed interiors and realistic lighting.

Motorcycles

Players can expect a variety of motorcycles suitable for city streets and highways throughout Leonida.

Boats and Watercraft

Since Vice City is surrounded by water, boats and personal watercraft will likely play an important role in exploration and missions.

More Than Just Cars

Official footage also shows pickup trucks, SUVs and other everyday vehicles, making Leonida feel realistic and diverse. Rockstar's attention to detail suggests players will have plenty of transportation choices.

Final Thoughts

Vehicles remain one of GTA's biggest attractions. As Rockstar reveals more information, GamePulse will continue reporting every official update about GTA VI's growing collection of cars, boats and other vehicles.
