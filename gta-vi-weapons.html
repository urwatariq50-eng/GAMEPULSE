GTA VI Weapons: Official Details

Rockstar Games has shown several weapons in official trailers, indicating a wide selection for different combat situations.

Handguns

Compact pistols appear to remain a reliable choice for close-range encounters and general gameplay.

Assault Rifles

The trailers also feature modern rifles suitable for intense action sequences and larger combat scenarios.

Shotguns

Shotguns continue to appear in GTA VI, offering powerful close-range performance during missions.

Combat and Strategy

Rockstar's trailers suggest that combat will feature improved animations, smarter AI and more tactical encounters. Players are expected to have a variety of weapon choices for different situations throughout the game.

Final Thoughts

Although Rockstar Games has not revealed the complete weapon list, the official footage already points to an exciting and varied combat system. GamePulse will continue covering every confirmed weapon update as more information is announced.
