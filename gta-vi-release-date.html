GTA VI Release Date: Everything Officially Confirmed

Published by GamePulse • July 2026

Grand Theft Auto VI is one of the most anticipated video games ever developed. After years of speculation, Rockstar Games officially revealed the next chapter of the Grand Theft Auto series, generating worldwide excitement among players. Every official announcement has attracted millions of viewers and reinforced the franchise's reputation as one of gaming's biggest names.

This article covers the information officially confirmed by Rockstar Games while clearly separating confirmed details from community speculation.

Rockstar Games Officially Reveals GTA VI

Rockstar Games introduced Grand Theft Auto VI through its first official trailer, giving players their first look at the game's setting, characters and visual improvements. The reveal quickly became one of the most watched gaming announcements in recent years.

Rockstar continues to be the most reliable source for information about GTA VI, and fans should rely on official announcements rather than unverified online rumors.

Return to Vice City

GTA VI takes players back to the iconic Vice City, now reimagined as part of the fictional state of Leonida. The new world features modern urban environments, beaches, highways, neighborhoods and surrounding countryside, all designed with significantly greater detail than previous entries in the series.

The official trailer showcases improved lighting, realistic weather effects, dense traffic and a vibrant world filled with activity, suggesting Rockstar's largest and most ambitious open-world experience to date.

Meet Jason and Lucia

The game follows two main protagonists, Jason and Lucia. Their relationship appears to play a central role in the story, with the trailer showing them working together during various criminal activities across Leonida.

Release Information

Rockstar Games has confirmed that Grand Theft Auto VI is scheduled to launch on PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X|S. At the time of writing, Rockstar has not officially announced a release date for a PC version.

As with any major game release, players should rely on Rockstar Games' official announcements for accurate information. Rumors and unofficial leaks often circulate online, but they should not be treated as confirmed news.

Why GTA VI Is One of the Biggest Games Ever

The Grand Theft Auto franchise has shaped the open-world gaming genre for more than two decades. Every new entry raises expectations for storytelling, graphics, gameplay and innovation.

Based on the official trailers and announcements, GTA VI aims to deliver a larger, more detailed and more immersive experience than any previous Rockstar title.

Final Thoughts

Grand Theft Auto VI is already one of the most anticipated games in gaming history. While fans eagerly await more official announcements, Rockstar Games has already revealed enough to generate enormous excitement around the world.

GamePulse will continue covering every official GTA VI announcement, trailer, gameplay reveal and update as soon as new information becomes available.
